A Job Fair Website

This website is built by four-person team. My role is front-end developer.

About this project:

Our project was to design and develop a job fair website which was a benefit for all students to find a job. As a front-end developer, I was responsible for information collection and discussion with clients. I developed the homepage, student page and the rating page. I also used my back-end team members’ support to find specific data from the database.

In the techical field, This website includes a counterdown timer created with Javascript. In the rating and Q&A page, back-end memebers and I collaborated on finding questions data and insert the submit form data.
